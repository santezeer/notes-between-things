## Poems

So basically, theres many ways to do traditionally and modernly. So, I attempted first to make on how I feel and I think it came good, I had no help from anyone or anything. 
Here it is:

<div class="poem">

a leaf falls<br>

for which way it falls?<br> 

it does not know<br>

it falls in a haze<br>

shrouded and deep <br>

down it goes<br> 

for there, it suppose to go<br>

the leaf falls<br>

the sign point down<br>
